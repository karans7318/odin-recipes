Red Velvet Cupcakes

Description

Red Velvet Cupcakes topped off with coconut shavings and cream cheese?! This indeed is an explosion of flavor on your taste buds.

Never baked sugary delights before? That's okay! Just follow our recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 box red velvet cake mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 3/4 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk or buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line pans with cupcake liners
  3. Sift cake mix and set aside
  4. Combine eggs, oil, sour cream, milk, and vanilla extract into a bowl.
  5. Stir until smooth.
  6. Fill the cupcake liners 3/4 full
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes
  8. Allow to cool
  9. Top it off with coconut cream
  10. Enjoy this delicacy!

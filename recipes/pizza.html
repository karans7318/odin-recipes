Home-made Pepperoni Pizza

Description

When you think of food -- what are some things that come to mind? Let's agree pizza was most definitely part of the list. Let's get to cooking one from your kitchen!

Never baked a pizza before? That's okay! Just follow our recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. Stretch the pizza dough
  3. Add the pizza to a lightly oiled baking pan
  4. Add pizza sauce
  5. Add your toppings (cheeses and pepperoni)
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown
  7. Remove from oven and top it off with basil and parmesan cheese
  8. Allow it to cool before enjoying!

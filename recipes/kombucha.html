Kombucha

Description

Looking for a healthier alternative to alcohol? Try out our fermented friend - Kombucha!

Never fermented a beverage before? That's okay! Just follow our methods!

Ingredients

  • 14 (3L) cups water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 8 bags black or green tea
  • 2 cups unflavored kombucha
  • 1 SCOBY

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Dissolve sugar into it (off the heat) and allow tea bags to soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Cool to room temperature.
  3. With clean hands add the SCOBY to a clean plate.
  4. Cover for a few days securing it with a cloth and rubber band.
  5. Allow it to ferment in the dark at room temperature.
  6. Bottle the kombuhca and add sweetener of your choosing.
  7. Allow it to ferment in the dark fro 3-10 days.
  8. You're finally finished! Serve and enjoy!
